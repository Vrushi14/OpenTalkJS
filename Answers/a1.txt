I can provide you with samples of professional writing, but I must advise that the following examples may not be suitable for your specific needs due to copyright and licensing restrictions.

Instead, I can offer you some general tips and templates that you can use as a starting point:

Q1. Resignation Letter

Here's a sample resignation letter:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State ZIP Code]

[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Title]
[Company Name]
[Company Address]
[City, State ZIP Code]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

Please accept this letter as formal notice of my resignation from my position as [Your Position] at [Company Name], effective [Date of Last Day of Work].

I appreciate the opportunities I have had while working at [Company Name], and I am grateful for the experience and knowledge gained during my time here.

Thank you for your understanding, and I wish the company continued success in the future.

Sincerely,
[Your Signature]

Q2. Job Application Email

Here's a sample job application email:

Subject: Application for [Job Title] at [Company Name]

Dear [Hiring Manager's Name],

I am writing to express my interest in the [Job Title] position at [Company Name]. I came across the job listing on [Source] and was impressed by the company's mission and values.

With [Number] years of experience in [Industry/Field], I believe I would be a strong fit for this role. My skills and qualifications include [List your relevant skills and experiences].

I have attached my resume for your review. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my application and how I can contribute to the success of [Company Name].

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Sincerely,
[Your Signature]

Q3. Project Proposal

Here's a sample project proposal:

Title: [Project Title]
Project Overview:
[Company/Organization]
[Project Description]
Objective:
To [briefly state the objective of the project]

Scope of Work:
The scope of work for this project includes [list the specific tasks and deliverables].

Timeline:
The project is scheduled to be completed within [Timeframe] months.

Resources:
We estimate that we will need [Number] resources, including [List the specific resources needed].

Deliverables:
The final deliverable will be a [Type of deliverable], such as [Specific deliverable].

Budget:
We are requesting a budget of [$Amount] for the project.

Conclusion:

I believe this project has the potential to [briefly state the potential benefits of the project]. I am excited about the opportunity to work on this project and contribute to its success.
